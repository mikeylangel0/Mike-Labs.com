About Mikey

Michael "Mikey" Palacios-Baughman is a multi talented video producer, director and editor. As well as being skilled in the art of graphic design, web development and other new media methodology.


Mikey first became interested in production right before college when he experimented with making short videos mainly for the purpose of making his friends laugh. He's gone on to hone his craft during his time at UConn and now at Eastern Connecticut State University.


Mikey has produced hundreds of web videos, close to a dozen short films among many other projects. He hopes to one day be a film director and is currently writing three feature scripts.
