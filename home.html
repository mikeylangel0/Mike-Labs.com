Welcome to Mike-Labs, what can we make for you?


Mike-Labs is the production company of multi-talented video producer, Mikey Palacios-Baughman. Mikey has a great deal of experience for someone his age. He's produced, or helped produce, hundreds of web-videos and multiple short films. He is a trusted and reliable film producer, having already produced two with many more in the pipeline. He's known for making the impossible happen. He's managed to get locations, for free, in a matter of days. He's been able to cast the night before a shoot. He's also a skilled cinematographer and video editor. He knows how to make something look great. He is fully proficient with a wide variety of camera and audio equipment as well as multiple video editing softwares. We're confident any project you bring us will come out amazing.
